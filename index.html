Who am I?

I’m Brad Sanders and I’m a software developer.

In middle school I built websites for my teachers as communication platforms for them and the parents. Working my way through online documentation, I learned enough HTML and JavaScript to make static webpages they could post news and events on. I also built a roller coaster database for thrill rides in the south east, mostly as an excuse to visit as many theme parks as possible, so I could report back and “scientific" observations I’d made.

Rediscovering my passion for web development, I started teaching myself Ruby in December of 2014 and attended App Academy in June of 2015. My stack includes Rails and Backbone.js; I’m currently teaching myself React.

I understood the ‘magic’ of Rails by first learning raw SQL before using ActiveRecord. I wrote my own, lite versions of Rails and ActiveRecord with plain Ruby to better understand what goes on ‘under the hood’.

I am constantly working on side projects while I look for a full time position in San Francisco.
