Who am I?

Most recently I designed and built software for the high volume e-commerce interior design start up Dot&Bo. I worked with a team building a new e-commerce suite, including a content management system and a brand new storefront; both React applications. I also architected and built a headless Rails API to serve both.

In my free time, I’m continuously working on an application for a newly founded non-profit, The Slade Dozier Foundation. We are in early stages of development, but our mission is to raise money for research and to spread awareness of SUDC (Sudden Unexpected Death in Childhood), a condition that affected my nephew Slade in 2015.

I’m currently looking for new opportunities in San Francisco.
